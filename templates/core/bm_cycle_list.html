About BM Cycles

Business Mentor Cycles represent the four quarterly cycles each year for business mentoring activities:

  • Cycle 1: January - March
  • Cycle 2: April - June
  • Cycle 3: July - September
  • Cycle 4: October - December

You can create new cycles annually or add years to existing cycle numbers.
