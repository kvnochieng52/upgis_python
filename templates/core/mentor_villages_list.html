About Mentor-Village Assignments

Automatic Household Assignment: When a mentor is assigned to a village, all households in that village automatically belong to that mentor. This means:

  • Mentors can view and manage all households in their assigned villages
  • Training sessions and visits are automatically linked to the correct mentor
  • Reports and statistics are calculated based on assigned villages
